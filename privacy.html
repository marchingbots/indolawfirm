Website Confidentiality Notice

Information submitted through this website does not create a lawyer-client relationship, advocate-client relationship, retainer, or engagement with the Firm.

Prospective clients should not transmit confidential, privileged, commercially sensitive, or time-sensitive information through website forms, email enquiries, or other communications until the Firm has confirmed its engagement and completed any required conflict checks.

The Firm's receipt of information from a prospective client does not create any obligation to act for that person or organisation and does not prevent the Firm from acting for another client where permitted by applicable law and professional obligations.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy and Confidentiality

We recognise that individuals and organisations who engage legal services often provide highly sensitive personal, financial, commercial, corporate, regulatory, and legal information.

The Firm is committed to protecting that information through appropriate legal, organisational, administrative, and technical safeguards.

Our lawyers and legal professionals are subject to professional duties of confidentiality and professional secrecy under Indonesian law, including Law No. 18 of 2003 concerning Advocates and the Indonesian Advocate Code of Ethics.

Nothing in this Privacy Statement is intended to limit any broader confidentiality obligations that may apply under applicable law, professional rules, or contractual arrangements.
