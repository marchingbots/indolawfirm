Professional Memberships & Admissions

Yafet Rissy & Partners Law Firm operates under the laws of the Republic of Indonesia.

The Firm's Indonesian advocates are admitted to practice law in Indonesia and are members of the Indonesian Advocates Association (PERADI). In providing legal services, they are subject to applicable Indonesian laws, professional obligations, and ethical standards governing the legal profession.

International Legal Professionals

International legal professionals associated with the Firm are members of the Indonesian Advocates Association (PERADI) while remaining subject to the professional obligations, ethical standards, and regulatory requirements of the jurisdictions and professional bodies to which they belong.

Compliance-First Practice

The Firm adopts a compliance-first approach to legal services. We seek to provide practical legal solutions while maintaining compliance with applicable laws, professional obligations, ethical standards, and regulatory requirements.

Our commitment is to responsible legal practice, professional integrity, and lawful outcomes.

Professional Indemnity Insurance

The Firm maintains professional indemnity insurance as part of its broader risk-management and professional responsibility framework.
